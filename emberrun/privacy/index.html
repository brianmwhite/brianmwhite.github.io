EmberRun Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 17, 2025

EmberRun does not collect or transmit any personal data from users. All data entered into the app—including saved runs, race predictions, and settings—is stored locally on your device or in your iCloud Drive (if backup is enabled).

We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not collect names, email addresses, or location data.

If you choose to contact us via a support form, any information you provide will be used solely to respond to your inquiry and will not be shared or stored beyond that purpose.

Your data is your own, and we take that seriously.

If you have any questions, please reach out via our support form.
